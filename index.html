FAQ

Will this annoy my clients?

No. ChaseAI uses polite, professional language that gets softer or firmer based on how overdue the invoice is. Most clients don't even realize it's automated.

What if a client already paid?

ChaseAI automatically stops reminders when you mark an invoice as paid. You can also pause or cancel any reminder at any time.

Can I customize the emails?

Yes! You can edit every template, adjust the tone, and set custom schedules. Pro users get AI-powered tone matching based on your writing style.

Does it integrate with my invoicing tool?

We integrate with QuickBooks, FreshBooks, Wave, and Xero. You can also add invoices manually or via our API.
